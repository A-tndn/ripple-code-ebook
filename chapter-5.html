‚ö†Ô∏è Common Logging Mistakes

Avoid these pitfalls that derail most beginners:

‚ùå Mistake 1: Only Logging Dramatic Events

Small ripples create the most consistent patterns. Dramatic events are outliers that distort your data.

‚ùå Mistake 2: Inconsistent Format

Changing your tracking format makes pattern analysis impossible. Stick to one structure.

‚ùå Mistake 3: Emotional Interpretation

Log facts, not feelings. "Sarah seemed upset" is interpretation. "Sarah replied with one-word answers" is data.

‚ùå Mistake 4: Perfectionism Paralysis

Better to log imperfectly than not at all. You can refine your method as you build the habit.
